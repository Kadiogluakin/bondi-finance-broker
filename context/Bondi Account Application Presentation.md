Compliance
We operate in compliance with Panamanian laws
Tokenization of Securities Is Not Regulated 
• Panama has no Virtual Asset Service Provider VASP) framework or legal requirements for crypto-related activities. 
• Tokenization and distribution of bonds do not fall under Panama’s financial, banking, or securities laws. 
• No licensing or regulatory obligations apply to companies engaged in crypto-asset transactions or securities tokenization. 
• Panama’s Superintendency of Banks SBP) and Superintendency of Securities Market SMV) confirmed they lack authority over 
crypto-related activities. 
 Not Subject to Panama’s AML/CFT Regulations 
• Crypto-related platforms, including security tokenization, are NOT designated as Financial or Non-Financial Reporting Entities under 
Law 23 of 2015. 
• Bondi Technologies Inc. is not required to register with Panamanian AML authorities and is not subject to AML/CFT supervision. 
• Panama’s legal framework does not extend anti-money laundering compliance obligations to tokenization or crypto-related services.
